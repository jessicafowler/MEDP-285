Characters

♦Carmela Soprano

Carmela Soprano is the wife of Tony Soprano, the mob boss of the Soprano family. Carmela deals with Tony's unfaithfulness and lifestyle while trying to raise two teenagers. SHe has ambitions and goals of her own; learning real estate and even taking on a housing project. She becomes confident enough to not be afraid to say what she wants in her relationship.

♦Meadow Soprano

Meadow Soprano is the daughter or Tony and Carmela Soprano. She is an ambitious girl who goes from a bratty teenager to a mature young woman studying medicine and law at Columbia University and living on her own in NYC. She has a large character development throughout the show, even when she is not present in the family hometown of NJ where most of the set takes place.

♦Adriana La Cerva

Adriana La Cerva was financee to an important character in the Sopranos, Christopher Moltisanti. He is the cousin of Tony Soprano and is very involved in Tony's life and business. Adriana is an extremely supportive woman, but gets herself into trouble when an FBI agent blackmails her and she is forced to be a rat or face time herself. She was very lonely and had no friends her own age, and longed for a future beyond a "mafia wife" lifestyle. Unfortunately, her compliance with the FBI cost her her life.
