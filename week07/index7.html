SOFTBALL

Benefits of playing

Softball is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams on a small field. It's a variation of baseball, but with a larger ball, underhand pitches, and a shorter game length.

Improves cardiovascular health, strength, and coordination Boosts mood, reduces stress, and enhances mental resilience Helps build social networks and fosters a sense of belonging Develops a range of skills that are useful both on and off the field
