Lasagna as a whole:

Lasagna is a delicious and popular Italian meal consitsing of three major components: noodles, sauce, and cheese. Of course mnay other components can be added to personalize your layers, including meat like sausage or ground beef and vegtables including zucchini and eggplant.

Combining these ingredients by layering them in a repeating pattern is the basis of a lasagna. Usually put in a casserole dish, this meal is then cooked in the oven at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
